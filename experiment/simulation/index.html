Instructions
To use Colour Blindness Simulator
  • You can choose any image and click on it.
  • After selecting the image, you can choose the button specific to a type of colour blindness to see how that image will appear to a person with that type of colour blindness.
  • You can also add images from your desktop to see how it will look like in different cases of colour blindess.
